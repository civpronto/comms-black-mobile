      <h1>Module 05 — Secure Communications (COMMSEC)</h1>
      <p class="meta">
        Level: Core · Recommended for: Shield / Shadow / Ghost · Est. time: ~20 minutes
      </p>

    <section>
  <h2>1. What COMMSEC Means</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Protecting the confidentiality and integrity of communications.</li>
    <li>Includes voice, messaging, file transfer and signalling metadata.</li>
    <li>Encryption is necessary but not sufficient — identity and metadata still matter.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>2. Encrypted App Suite</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>End-to-end encrypted messengers for content protection.</li>
    <li>Profile and contact settings tuned for reduced data exposure.</li>
    <li>Per-identity apps on different profiles/devices where needed.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>3. PGP &amp; High-Assurance Channels</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>PGP for email or file exchange when long-term verifiability matters.</li>
    <li>Key management disciplines: revocation, backups, offline storage.</li>
    <li>When to step up from app-level encryption to PGP-grade workflows.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>4. Behavioural Rules</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Keep sensitive conversations on consistent, compartmented channels.</li>
    <li>Never mix high-risk and personal chat in the same app identity.</li>
    <li>Assume contacts may screenshot, forward or leak — plan accordingly.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
