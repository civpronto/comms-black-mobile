Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a subscription?

No. Comms Black Mobile does not charge a subscription. Some features rely on third‑party providers (e.g., Silent.link for data eSIMs, Blacktel for VoIP, Starlink for satellite internet). Ongoing costs come directly from those providers.

What’s included with each communication kit?

Every kit includes a GrapheneOS phone, protective accessories, charging equipment, and, depending on the package, privacy hardware and anonymous connectivity.

  • Anonymous/Operator/Sovereign Aussie: include starter credits for data and VoIP to get you going.
  • Everyday Aussie: designed for customers using their own SIM.
How do I recharge data or VoIP credit?

Recharge directly through the provider (Silent.link / Blacktel / Starlink). We can guide you through the process or handle it if you provide funds.

How much do ongoing services cost?

Prices change over time. Expect roughly market rates for data eSIMs, VoIP numbers, and satellite plans. We’ll provide current rates during ordering.

Can I use my own SIM card?

Yes. Everyday Aussie is designed for your own SIM. Other kits can also use your SIM, though some privacy features rely on anonymous eSIMs.

Is GrapheneOS difficult to use?

No. It feels familiar if you’ve used Android. The difference is hardened security and more control.

What support do you provide?

We’ll help with setup, using your kit, and managing providers. You’ll always know how to recharge data, VoIP or satellite services.
