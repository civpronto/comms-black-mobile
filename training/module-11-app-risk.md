<section>
  <h2>üì± 1. How Apps Leak Data</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Tracking SDKs and analytics frameworks.</li>
    <li>Permissions for contacts, calendars, sensors and location.</li>
    <li>Background network requests for updates, ads and profiling.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>üìù 2. App Vetting Checklist</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Who makes this app and what is their business model?</li>
    <li>What permissions does it request, and are they justified?</li>
    <li>Does it still work with tighter network and sensor restrictions?</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>‚úÖ 3. Safer App Patterns</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Prefer well-maintained apps with transparent privacy information.</li>
    <li>Minimise the number of apps installed, especially in high-risk roles.</li>
    <li>Review app lists regularly and remove what you no longer use.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>üß© 4. Per-Kit Strategy</h2>

    <li>Ghost: minimum viable set for the specific operation.</li>
  </ul>
