    <section>
  <h2>1. How Apps Leak Data</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Tracking SDKs and analytics libraries.</li>
    <li>Contact, location and sensor access.</li>
    <li>Background network requests you never see.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>2. App Vetting Checklist</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Who publishes it and what else do they make?</li>
    <li>What permissions does it request and why?</li>
    <li>Does it still work with network and sensor restrictions?</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>3. Safer App Categories</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Open-source where possible, or at least well-audited.</li>
    <li>Minimal permissions, clear privacy policy, no dark patterns.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>4. Per-Kit App Strategy</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Shield: broader but vetted app set.</li>
    <li>Shadow: essential apps only, with strict permissions.</li>
    <li>Ghost: minimal set required for specific operations.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
